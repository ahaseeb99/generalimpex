WELCOME TO GENERAL IMPLEX

General Impex a parent company in International trade since 1977. Company has made its mark globally by meeting international quality standards and high competitiveness for its core product range. Our product range includes white rice, parboiled rice, red lentil/masoor dal, sesame seed, spices and herbs. Strict quality and hygiene standards, competitive prices, on-time delivery and customer satisfaction are our key focus areas.

COMPANY Profile

With more than 35 years of experience in the Pakistan industry, we have gained a competitive edge in Best price by selecting high quality raw material directly from farmers in each particular area of Pakistan. Flexibility by offering different packing weights of your choice and accepting made-to-order requests we have been established in the agri business as a high quality and reliable supplier and distributor.

Our mission is to provide value-added products and services to our customer's. Believing in the motto that the customer has a proven right to demand the finest product, our endeavor over the last three decade of glorious effort have always been towards creating and providing the best. Perhaps it is this product excellence and perfectionist approach, which have enabled us to build and maintain a longstanding and strong relationship with our customers.

A highly qualified and dedicated team of professionals form a formidable back-up at General Impex to ensure an unimpeded flow of goods and services in precision to the discerning buyer. All operations are constantly monitored and managed by this finely honed and experienced group, which ensures that the Company retains and excels further in building up its reputation. General Impex growth has been rapid and steady and after successfully establishing strong grip in the domestic market several popular brands in the domestic market, the administrative board decided to introduce General Impex as an Exporter to the International market. Its aim is to built up a world wide reputation for the quality of its Agri products exports to major markets in Asia, Middle East, Europe & Africa. With more than 35 years experience in Agri business and a commitment in providing best services, we can ensure that our customers will receive the consistency high quality products with reasonable price. Since the raw material selection process until the end of production line, our team has supervised in every stage to make sure that products from our company are clean and correct specification as required. The global Agri trade is very complex. Especially regarding the difference between standard and quality Agro product. This has prompted General Impex to make constant improvements and to move consistently into the quality sector. The Company's achievement in the face of such strong competition rests on the four pillars of QUALITY, EXPERIENCE, HONESTY AND PROMPT SERVICE. The same consistent performance and trustworthy services.
